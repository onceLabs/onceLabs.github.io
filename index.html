FarpointeSDK

Easily interface with Farpointe Readers

Overview

FarpointeSDK is used to manage the registration, activation, and deactivation of associated Access Cards with relevant Farpointe Reader devices. This library abstracts the complex concepts of NeonFiles, Synchronization, and other Legic-related processes into a simple interface.

Use the shared instance of FarpointeSdk for various actions:

  • register the user’s device with the Legic Connect Server

  • register a FarpointeAccessCard

  • activate a FarpointeAccessCard

  • deactivate a FarpointeAccessCard

  • And More. See FarpointeSdkProviding
